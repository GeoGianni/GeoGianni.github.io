Introduction

Hello, my name is Gianni Rivero and I am a self taught Software Developer with over 12 years of experience. I'm also a Indie Unity game developer. I've been interested in computers since as early as I can remember; from my 10 year-old self loading up custom WarCraft III maps trying to understand what made them tick, to engineering and robotics classes. I learned Unity3D and Blender at 12 and created and lead my high school's competetive programming club at 17. It seems no matter where I was in life, I was trying to understand and create whatever I could.

As I got older I began to take interest in more complex design. I began learning to create game systems and semi-complex AI, creating levels and worlds using procedural generation and interconnected interactions. When I finished high school I decided to go to school to study computer science. While my college experience was unique and taught me a lot, after two years I learned it wasn't for me. I had multiple jobs throughout my education and all of them failed to keep my focus. I knew if I was to be sucessful and earn a living doing something, It was going to be doing what I loved.

This is where it all began.

Purpose

This website is meant to be a short-term project, a porfolio to show off to potential employers, but mostly it is a benchmark. This website is a promise to myself to display all I have worked towards and even if it is never seen by anyone else but me, it will be a constant reminder of the effort I have put into learning what inspires me.
